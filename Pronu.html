Pronu

Description

A group project involving the creation of a mission statement, tagline, core values, and designed brandmark for a hypothetical plant-based proteins company.

Competition

We began by researching our fiercest competitors for plant-based proteins. We determined our main competition was Meta Burger, for their affordable products. After looking at the ingredients for different products, we realized other competitors used numerous ingredients to create a “meaty” texture, evolving into a more expensive product.

In order to stand out among our competition, we had to make affordable prices be one of our main goals. Other competitive aspects to consider included recognizability and availability. For example, Red Robin offers the impossible burger for $3.50.

Personas

We developed four personas to help us determine who our target audience might be. We created a positive persona, present persona, neutral persona, and a negative persona. This range of personas helped us discover the different range of needs our consumers may have, and even what the needs are of personas who wouldn’t normally purchase our product, but may be willing under the right conditions.

Naming Ideation

When choosing a name, we tried to choose common names that can easily be identified as an alternative meat. We found this resulted in names that weren’t memorable, so we decided to work with two keywords: protein and nourish. This resulted in the name Pronu.

Mission Statement

After having a recent idea of our goals and a start on our brand identity, we moved onto our Mission Statement, along with our Vision, Core Values, Objectives, and our Tagline.

Our Mission Statement underwent a number of trials before reaching our final version. We knew that our product would be more likely to succeed using less ingredients, as this would cut prices and inspire a brand of authenticity and transparency. We first focused less on processing, and then moved to focusing on lower income communities. This choice would ideally bring a nutritious meat alternative without the extra cost, while opening our business to an audience base that differs from other similar brands.
