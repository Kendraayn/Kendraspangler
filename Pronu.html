Pronu

Description

Develop a mission statement, tagline, core values, and designed brandmark for a plant-based proteins company.

Competition

After reviewing our competitors, we determined our main competition was Meta Burger, for their affordable products. After looking at the ingredients for different products, we realized other competitors used numerous ingredients to create a meaty texture, leading to a more expensive product.

In order to stand out among our competition, we had to make affordable prices be one of our main goals. We also considered recognizability and availability. For example, Red Robin offers the impossible burger for $3.50.

Personas

Mission Statement

Our Mission Statement underwent a number of trials before reaching our final version. We knew that our product would be more likely to succeed using less ingredients, as this would cut prices and inspire a brand of authenticity and transparency. We then turned our focus on less processing and lower income communities. This choice would ideally bring a nutritious meat alternative without the extra cost, while opening our business to an audience base that differs from other similar brands.

The blending of business needs and visual design feels unique with this project in particular. If given more time to work on the project, I would want to advance and fine tune the goals of the company even further and develop more promotional works building on the core values of the company.
